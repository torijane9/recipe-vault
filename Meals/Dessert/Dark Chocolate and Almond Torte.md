### Dark Chocolate and Almond Torte 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 160g good quality dark chocolate, chopped coarsely
- [ ] 160g butter
- [ ] 5 eggs, sesparated
- [ ] 165g (3/4 cup) caster sugar
- [ ] 125g (1 cup) ground almonds
- [ ] 50g (2/3 cup) roasted flaked almonds, chopped coarsely
- [ ] 35g (1/3 cup) coarsely grated good quality dark chocolate
- [ ] [[chocolate ganache]]
---
#### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Grease a deep 22cm round cake pan, line base and sides with two layers of baking paper.
2. Stir chopped chocolate and butter in a small saucepan over low heat until smooth. Cool to room temperature.
3. Beat egg yolks and sugar in a small bowl with an electric mixer until thick and creamy. 
4. Transfer egg yolks and sugar to a large bowl, and fold in the chocolate mixture, ground almonds, flaked almonds and grated chocolate.
5. Beat egg whites in a separate bowl with an electric mixer until soft peaks form. Fold into chocolate mixture, in two batches. Pour mixture into lined cake pan.
6. Bake cake for about 45 minutes. Stand cake in the pan for at least 15 minutes before turning onto a wire rack. Turn top-side up to cool.
7. Spread chocolate ganache over cake.
