### Lemon Cheesecake

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
Base
- [ ]  250g packet of plain biscuits (wine biscuits, malt biscuits, gingernuts, etc)
- [ ] 120g butter, melted
Filling
- [ ] 2 packets lemon jelly
- [ ] 1 cup boiling water
- [ ] 2 tsp lemon zest
- [ ] 1/4 cup lemon juice
- [ ] 250g cream cheese, softened at room temp.
- [ ] 225g (1 cup) caster sugar
- [ ] 340ml can evaporated milk
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla extract
---
#### Instructions
1. To make the base, crush biscuits into fine fine crumbs. Combine thoroughly with melted butter and press into loose-bottom tin. Bake at 180°C for 10 minutes. Leave to cool.
2. Dissolve jelly in boiling water. Add lemon zest and juice, and allow to cool. 
3. In a separate bowl, combine the softened cream cheese with the sugar
4. In a mixing bowl, whisk evaporated milk until thick. Slowly add the cream cheese and sugar mixture to the evaporated milk, followed by the vanilla extract, and mix thoroughly so there are no lumps of cream cheese.
5. Mix in the jelly mixture (make sure it’s not hot, but if it’s still a bit warm that’s okay).
6. Pour the filling onto the biscuit base, and chill for at least 3 hours. 

#### Notes:
- Mixing the cream cheese and sugar together in a separate bowl before adding to the evaporated milk makes it easier to combine everything together without any lumps of cream cheese, but it is an extra dish to wash so it isn’t an essential step.
