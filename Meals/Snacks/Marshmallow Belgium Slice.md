### Marshmallow Belgium Slice 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1/2 cup white sugar
- [ ] 100g butter
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 1 cup plain flour
- [ ] 1 tsp baking powder
- [ ] 1 tsp mixed spice
- [ ] 2 tsp cinnamon
- [ ] 1 packet jelly crystals
- [ ] 1 Tbsp gelatine
- [ ] 2 cups water
- [ ] 1 cup white sugar
---
#### Instructions
1. Base: Preheat oven to 160 degrees c. Line a slice tin with baking paper (about 20cmx30cm).
2. Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add the egg, beating well.
3. Sift together the flour, baking powder, and spices, and fold in to the creamed mixture.
4. Press into slice tin, and chill for 10 minutes, then bake for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through. Cool.
5. Marshmallow: Combine jelly crystals, gelatine, and water in a large pot, and boil together for 8 minutes.
6. Add sugar, stir well to dissolve completely, and remove from the heat. Allow to cool until warm enough to touch.
7. Beat with an electric mixer until thick (timing will vary, normally from 5-10 minutes).
8. Pour marshmallow over the cooled base, and set in the fridge for at least 2 hours.
