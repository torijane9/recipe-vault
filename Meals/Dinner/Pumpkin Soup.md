### Pumpkin Soup

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1 large onion
- [ ] neutral oil
- [ ] 3 garlic cloves
- [ ] butter
- [ ] curry powder
- [ ] paprika
- [ ] mixed herbs
- [ ] salt
- [ ] pepper
- [ ] pumpkin, peeled and cut up
- [ ] water or stock (chicken or vegetable)
- [ ] 1/2 can coconut cream
---
#### Instructions
1. Peel and roughly chop onion.
2. Heat a big pot or saucepan (enough to fit all the ingredients) on medium, and add the onion, garlic, butter, curry powder, paprika, mixed herbs, salt and pepper, and stir together.
3. Add the pumpkin, and stir everything together. Fry for a couple of minutes, the add water/stock so that the pumpkin is covered.
4. Cook until pumpkin is soft. 
5. Take off the heat, and blend together with stick blender. Taste and adjust seasonings or spices if needed.
6. Add the coconut cream, mix together, and enjoy
