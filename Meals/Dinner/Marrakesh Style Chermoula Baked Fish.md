#### Instructions
1. Make incisions across both sides of the body of the fish and rub the chermoula paste in well. Stuff the body cavity with the fresh herbs.
2. Place in a shallow baking dish, pour extra chermoula paste over the fish and cover with slices of lemon, tomato, onion, and a few olives.
3. Drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.
4. Bake in the oven at 180Â° for 10-15 minutes until cooked.
5. Serve with couscous
