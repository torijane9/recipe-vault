### Fish Parcels with Coconut Rice

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] [[Coconut Rice]]
- [ ] Baking paper cut into 4 sheets, big enough to wrap around fish fillets
- [ ] Neutral oil
- [ ] 4x 150g firm white fish fillets
- [ ] 1 large garlic clove, minced
- [ ] 1 thumb-sized knob of fresh garlic, grated
- [ ] 1 tsp fish sauce
- [ ] 2 tsp sesame oil
- [ ] 1/2 fresh red chilli, finely chopped
- [ ] 2 spring onions, thinly sliced on diagonal
- [ ] Salt and pepper, to taste
- [ ] Coriander leaves , for serving
- [ ] 1 lime, quartered, for serving
---
#### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Cut baking paper into 4 sheets, big enough to wrap around the fish fillets. On a clean surface, place sheets side by side and brush them lightly with oil. Place a fish fillet on each. 
2. Combine garlic, ginger, fish sauce, sesame oil, and chilli in a small bowl. Brush/spoon over each of the fish fillets, then sprinkle spring onion over top. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Fold the paper in half over the fish and twist the edges of the paper together to each each parcel. Place the parcels side by side on an oven tray and bake for 6-8 minutes or until the fish is just cooked. 
4. Open the parcels at the table and serve with coriander leaves, lime wedges, and coconut rice.
