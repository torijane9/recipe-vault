### Soy & Ginger Boneless Short Rib Bao Buns 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 3 cups shredded green cabbage
- [ ] 1 bunch radishes, cleaned and thinly sliced
- [ ] 3 bok choy heads, thinly sliced
- [ ] 1 medium carrot, julienned
- [ ] 1 Tbsp sesame seeds
- [ ] 2 Tbsp honey
- [ ] Juice of 2 limes
- [ ] 1/4 cup white vinegar
- [ ] Salt and pepper to taste
- [ ] Chilli flakes to taste
- [ ] [[soy & ginger boneless short ribs]]
- [ ] [[steamed bao buns]]

---
#### Instructions
1. In a large bowl, combine the cabbage, radishes, bok choy, carrot.
2. In a measuring jug, mix together the sesame seeds, honey, lime juice, white vinegar, chilli flakes, salt, and pepper.
3. Add the dressing to the vegetables. Mix well and set aside.
4. To assemble, place a spoonful of the short ribs into warm bao buns and top with the slaw. Enjoy!
