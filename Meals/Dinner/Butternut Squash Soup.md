## Butternut Squash Soup

### Ingredients
- [ ] 2 cups [[Butternut Squash Purée]]
- [ ] 1/3 - 1/2 cup chicken stock, depending on preference
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Smoked paprika
---
### Instructions
1. Place the butternut squash purée and chicken stock in a small saucepan over medium-low heat and whisk until smooth.
2. Simmer for 15-20 minutes. Season with salt to taste, and garnish with a pinch of smoked paprika.
