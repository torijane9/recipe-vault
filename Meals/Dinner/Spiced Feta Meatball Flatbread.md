## Spiced Feta Meatball Flatbread

### Ingredients
- [ ] Olive oil
- [ ] 1-2 large handfuls of thinly sliced cabbage
- [ ] kosher salt
- [ ] 6-7 [[Spiced Feta Meatballs]]
- [ ] [[Garlicky Yoghurt Sauce]]
- [ ] Dill, for garnish
- [ ] Crumbled feta, for garnish
Garlic Flatbreads
- [ ] 2 flatbread rounds, reheated
- [ ] 1 Tbsp butter
- [ ] 1 clove garlic, grated
---
### Instructions
1. Heat 1-2 Tbsp olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Once hot, add cabbage and a small pinch of salt. Sauté until cabbage is wilted and slightly caramelised.
2. Add spiced feta meatballs, reduce the heat to low and sauté for 3-4 minutes.
3. In the meantime, prepare the garlic flatbreads: heat 1 Tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add flatbreads and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until fully reheated.
4. In a small microwave-safe bowl, add the butter and garlic. microwave for 30 seconds, or until the butter is melted and the garlic is fragrant. Stir until well combined.
5. Lightly brush the garlic butter over each reheated flatbread.
6. Plate the flatbreads, and top with the sautéed meatballs and cabbage.
7. Garnish with garlicky yoghurt sauce, dill, and crumbled feta.
