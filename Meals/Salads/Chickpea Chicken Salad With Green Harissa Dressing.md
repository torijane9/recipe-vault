## Chickpea Chicken Salad With Green Harissa Dressing

### Ingredients
- [ ] [[Green Harissa Dressing]]
- [ ] 2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- [ ] 1 can chickpeas, drained
- [ ] 1 tsp ground cumin
- [ ] 1 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
- [ ] 1/4 tsp ground chilli powder (optional)
- [ ] 2 cups arugula or rocket
- [ ] 1/2 cup roughly torn fresh coriander leaves, and more for garnish
- [ ] 2 heaped cups boneless cooked chicken (such as rotisserie, grilled, or poached), roughly chopped or torn and seasoned lightly with salt
- [ ] 1/2 cup cherry-tomato halves, seasoned lightly with salt
- [ ] 1/2 cup pitted Kalamata olives
---
### Instructions
1. Heat the olive oil in a medium skillet over a medium heat. Toss the chickpeas in the cumin, paprika, salt, and chilli powder, if using, then add to the skilled.
2. Fry the chickpeas until crispy. Remove from the heat and keep covered until ready to serve. 
3. To assemble the salad arrange the arugula and coriander in a large shallow bowl. Scatter the chicken and the chickpeas on top of the leaves. Top with the tomatoes and olives, and garnish with more coriander.
4. Serve with the dressing on top or on the side.
