## Warm Roast Kumara Salad

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1kg red, gold, orange kumara
- [ ] 1 red onion, peeled and cut into wedges
- [ ] 1 red capsicum, thickly sliced
- [ ] 3 Tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 2 Tbsp lemon juice
- [ ] 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- [ ] 2 tsp ground cumin
- [ ] 2 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] salt, to taste
- [ ] freshly ground black pepper, to taste
- [ ] 2 Tbsp chopped parsley
- [ ] [[Feta & Lemon Dressing]]
---
### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200c. Line a large oven dish with baking paper.
2. Cut the kumara into wedges and place them in a large bowl with the onion and capsicum. Add the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, capsicum, and paprika. Mix and season to taste.
3. Bake the vegetables in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes until the vegetables are golden and tender. Set the vegetables aside to cool, then place them in a large bowl.
4. Drizzle the feta and lemon dressing over the vegetables and toss gently to combine. Sprinkle with the chopped parsley
