### Ultimate Salmon Bagel 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1 bagel of choice
- [ ] 1-2 scoops of [[Classic Bagel Schmear]]
- [ ] 2-3 slices cold smoked salmon
- [ ] Spoonful of [[Pickled Red Onions]]
---
#### Instructions
1. Slice and toast your bagel.
2. Spread a generous layer of [[Classic Bagel Schmear]] over the bottom bagel half.
3. Lay slices of cold smoked salmon, and follow with the [[Pickled Red Onions]].
4. Add the bagel top, and enjoy!
