### Chia Yoghurt Breakfast Pudding Bowls

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1 cup yoghurt of choice
- [ ] 1/2 cup milk of choice
- [ ] 1/2 cup frozen fruit of choice
- [ ] OR 1/2 cup fruit puree of choice
- [ ] 1-3 tsp sweetener (optional)
- [ ] 3 Tbsp chia seeds
Topping Ideas
- [ ] nut butter
- [ ] [[Granola]]
- [ ] fresh or frozen fruit
- [ ] nuts or seeds
---
#### Instructions
1. If using frozen fruit: combine yoghurt, milk, frozen fruit, and sweetener (if using) in a blender. Blitz until smooth.
2. If using fruit puree: mix yoghurt, milk, fruit puree, and sweetener (if using) together until thoroughly combined.
3. Pour into jar or bowl and add the chia seeds. Whisk to combine.
4. Cover and refrigerate overnight and give it a good stir in the morning.
5. Add all your favourite toppings at enjoy!
