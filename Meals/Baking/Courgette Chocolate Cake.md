### Courgette Chocolate Cake

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 2 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
- [ ] 3/4 cup cocoa
- [ ] 2 tsp baking soda
- [ ] 1/2 tsp baking powder
- [ ] 1 tsp instant coffee
- [ ] 1/2 tsp salt
- [ ] 1 cup neutral oil (eg. canola or vegetable)
- [ ] 1 cup granulated sugar
- [ ] 3/4 cup loosely packed brown sugar
- [ ] 4 large eggs
- [ ] 3/4 cup buttermilk (or regular milk + 1 tsp vinegar)
- [ ] 2 tsp vanilla extract
- [ ] 3 cups grated courgette (about 3 medium)
- [ ] 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
---
#### Instructions
1. Mix flour, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, instant coffee, and salt together.
2. In a separate bowl mix oil, sugars, eggs, buttermilk, vanilla, and courgette.
3. Mix everything together and add chocolate chips.
