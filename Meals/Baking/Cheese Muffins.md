### Cheese Muffins

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 2 1/4 cup plain flour
- [ ] 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
- [ ] 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
- [ ] 1/2 tsp salt
- [ ] 200g (approx 2 cups) grated cheese
- [ ] 1 1/4 cup milk
- [ ] 1 tsp vinegar
- [ ] 1/2 cup neutral oil
- [ ] 2 eggs
- [ ] 1 garlic clove, minced
Optional additions
- [ ] herbs
- [ ] cream cheese
- [ ] chilli flakes
---
#### Instructions
1. Mix milk and vinegar together and leave for a few minutes.
2. Sieve together flour, baking powder, baking sofa, salt, and any other dry optional additions together in a bowl. 
3. Add grated cheese and combine, making sure the cheese isnâ€™t clumping together too much.
4. Combine the vinegar/milk mixture with the oil, eggs, minced garlic, and any other wet optional additions. 
5. Fold the two mixtures together, trying not to overmix.
