### Pear and Gingerbread Upside Down Pudding 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
Base
- [ ] 50g butter
- [ ] 1/2 cup brown sugar
- [ ] 4 small ripe pears, cored and halved lengthwise
Gingerbread
- [ ] 3/4 cup flour
- [ ] 1/2 tsp baking soda
- [ ] 1/4 tsp salt
- [ ] 2 tsp cinnamon
- [ ] 1 tsp ground ginger
- [ ] 1/4 tsp grated nutmeg
- [ ] 3/4 cup brown sugar
- [ ] 1/2 cup milk
- [ ] 1/4 cup golden syrup
- [ ] 50g butter, melted
- [ ] 1 egg, lightly beaten
---
#### Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C. Grease and line base and sides of a 20cm round cake tin.
2. Prepare the base. Stir the butter and sugar together in a saucepan, until the sugar has dissolved. Pour evenly over the base of the prepared cake tin and arrange the pears in the tin cut-side down.
3. Into a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ground ginger, and nutmeg and make a well in the centre.
4. Stir together the brown sugar, milk, golden syrup, butter, and egg and pour into the dry ingredients. Mix together, and pour the mixture carefully over the pears.
5. Bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until a cake skewer inserted in the middle comes out clean. Allow the cake to cool slightly before turning out onto a serving plate.
