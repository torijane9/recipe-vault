### Honey Ginger Slice

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 125g butter
- [ ] 2 Tbsp honey
- [ ] 1 1/2 cup plain flour
- [ ] 1 tsp mixed spice
- [ ] 2 tsp ground ginger
- [ ] 1 tsp baking soda
- [ ] 3/4 cup brown sugar
- [ ] 1 egg
---
#### Instructions
1. Melt butter and honey together, then add baking soda
2. Mix dry ingredients
3. Add the butter mix and egg, and stir to combine without over-mixing.
