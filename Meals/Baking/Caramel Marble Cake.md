### Caramel Marble Cake 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 125g butter
- [ ] 1 cup granulated sugar
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 1 Tbsp golden syrup
- [ ] 1 cup milk
- [ ] 1 tsp baking soda
- [ ] 1 1/2 cup flour
- [ ] 1 tsp baking powder
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla extract
- [ ] 1 Tbsp cocoa
- [ ] Your favourite chocolate frosting
---
#### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Grease and line a baking tin with greaseproof paper.
2. Cream the butter and sugar. Add egg and mix to combine.
3. In a measuring jug, add the milk and golden syrup, and warm in the microwave. Add baking soda, then combine with the butter mixture.
4. Add flour and baking powder, mix until just combined.
5. Pour half the mixture into the lined baking tin. 
6. Add cocoa to the remaining mixture, then pour into baking tin and swirl the two mixtures together.
7. Bake for approx. 30 minutes, or until a skewer in the centre comes out clean, and the cake springs back when touched.
