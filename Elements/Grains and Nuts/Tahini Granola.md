### Tahini Granola 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1/2 cup (120g) tahini
- [ ] 1/3 cup (115g) honey, depending on preferred sweetness can go up to 1/2 cup
- [ ] 3 Tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla extract
- [ ] 3/4 tsp cinnamon
- [ ] 1/2 tsp salt
- [ ] 3 cups rolled oats
- [ ] 3/4 cup nuts of choice (pecans, almond slivers, walnuts, hazelnuts, etc)
---
#### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 165 C and line a tray with baking paper.
2. In a small saucepan, combine tahini, honey, olive oil, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt. Stir over medium low heat until warm and combined (about 3 minutes or so). You can also microwave for 20-30 seconds.
3. In a large bowl, mix together oats and nuts. Pour the tahini mixture over the oats and stir until evenly combined.
4. Spread the mixture evenly onto baking tray. Bake for 20-25 minutes until light golden brown, stirring once halfway through. 
5. Cool completely, then break granola into desired cluster size.
6. Store in an airtight container, will last at least a couple months.
