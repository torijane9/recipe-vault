## Tinga Braising Sauce

### Ingredients
- [ ] Neutral oil
- [ ] 1 onion, sliced
- [ ] 3 large garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 1 can diced tomatoes
- [ ] 2-3 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, roughly chopped
- [ ] 1 cup chicken broth (can substitute water)
- [ ] 1 tsp dried oregano
- [ ] 1 tsp ground cumin
---
### Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and combine until smooth.
