### Red Wine Cassis Pears 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 220g (1 cup) granulated sugar
- [ ] 1 cup red wine
- [ ] 1/2 cup blackcurrant syrup
- [ ] 6 ripe pears, peeled
- [ ] 1-2 cinnamon sticks
- [ ] 1 orange, rind and juice
- [ ] (Optional) 1/2 cup crème de cassis
---
#### Instructions
1. Combine the sugar, wine, blackcurrant syrup, and crème de cassis (if using) in a pot and heat on low-medium, stirring occasionally until the sugar dissolves.
2. Add the pears, cinnamon, and orange rind and juice.
