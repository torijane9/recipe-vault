### Herbed Mini Pancakes 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 1/2 cup milk
- [ ] 1 tsp sugar
- [ ] 1/2 tsp salt
- [ ] 1 Tbsp chopped dill
- [ ] 1 Tbsp chopped chives
- [ ] 3/4 cup self-raising flour
---
#### Instructions
1. Combine egg and milk together in a bowl, then add the sugar, salt, and herbs.
2. Add self-raising flour and stir together until just combined.
3. Heat a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat.
4. Cook spoonfuls of mixture (about a tablespoon amount) until bubbles start to form on the top, then flip and cook for another 30 seconds or so until cooked through and the pancakes spring back if you press the top lightly.
