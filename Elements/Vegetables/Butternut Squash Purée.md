## Butternut Squash Purée

### Ingredients
- [ ] Approx 700g butternut squash, diced into small (approx 3cm) cubes
- [ ] 3/4 tsp kosher salt
- [ ] Olive oil
- [ ] 1 shallot, diced
- [ ] 3 Tbsp (45g) heavy cream
- [ ] 1/3 cup (80ml) chicken stock
- [ ] 1 Tbsp brown sugar
- [ ] 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] A pinch of cinnamon
- [ ] A pinch of cayenne pepper
---
### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200°C. In a mixing bowl, toss together the butternut squash, olive oil, and a small pinch of salt.
2. Transfer butternut squash to a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake for 30-35 minutes or until fully soft.
3. Heat olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat. Add shallot and a pinch of salt and sauté until soft and translucent (around 3-4 minutes).
4. Transfer cooked butternut squash and shallot to a blender. Add the rest of the ingredients and blend until smooth.
