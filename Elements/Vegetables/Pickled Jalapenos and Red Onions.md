## Pickled Jalapenos and Red Onions

### Ingredients
- [ ] 3 jalapenos, thinly sliced
- [ ] 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- [ ] 1/2 cup (110g) white sugar
- [ ] 1/2 cup (110g) apple cider vinegar
- [ ] 1 cup (220ml) water
- [ ] 2 tsp kosher salt
---
### Instructions
1. Place sliced jalapenos and red onion in a quart deli container or large jar.
2. In a small saucepan on medium-high heat, combine sugar, vinegar, water, and salt. Stir until sugar is mostly dissolved.
3. Once pickling liquid begins steaming, pour directly over jalapenos and red onion.
4. Allow everything to cool down to room temperature before sealing and storing in the fridge.
