### Avocado Cucumber Salsa

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1 ripe avocado, cubed
- [ ] 2 mini cucumbers, chopped
- [ ] 2 spring onions 
- [ ] Large bunch coriander, finely chopped
- [ ] 1 lime, juiced
- [ ] Salt, to taste
---
#### Instructions
1. Mix all of the ingredients together, seasoning with salt to taste.
2. Serve immediately.
