## Berry Ice Cubes

### Ingredients
- [ ] Water
- [ ] Fresh berries of choice (raspberries, blackberries, blueberries, etc)
- [ ] Mint leaves, washed
---
### Instructions
1. Add berries and mint leaves to an ice cube tray.
2. Top with water, and freeze until solid.
