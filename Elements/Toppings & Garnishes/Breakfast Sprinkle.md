### Breakfast Sprinkle
A great sprinkle for your breakfast cereal, porridge, yoghurt, or fruit parfait for more flavour and nutrients.
### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] Slivered almonds
- [ ] Toasted coconut flakes
- [ ] Coconut sugar
- [ ] Sunflower seeds
- [ ] Black sesame seeds
- [ ] Sunflower seeds
- [ ] Dried fruit of choice - blueberries, cranberries, apricots (chopped), dates (chopped) etc
---
#### Instructions
1. Thoroughly combine all the ingredients together in a bowl.
2. Store in an airtight container.
