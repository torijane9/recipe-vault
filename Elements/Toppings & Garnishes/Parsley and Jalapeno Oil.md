### Parsley and Jalapeno Oil

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley
- [ ] 1 Tbsp diced jalapeno pepper
- [ ] 2 tbsp oil of choice (olive, coconut, avocado, etc)
- [ ] pinch of salt
---
#### Instructions
1. Grind parsley and jalapeno together in a mortar. 
2. Drizzle in olive oil and season with salt; stir to combine.
