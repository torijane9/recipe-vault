### Frangipane Filling

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 125g unsalted butter, softened
- [ ] 125g caster sugar
- [ ] 2 large eggs
- [ ] 125g ground almonds
- [ ] 25g plain flour
- [ ] 1/2 tsp almond extract
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla extract
- [ ] Pinch of salt
---
#### Instructions
1.  In a medium-sized bowl, cream together the softened butter and caster sugar until light and fluffy. An electric mixer makes this quicker, but a wooden spoon works too!
2. Beat in eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition until fully incorporated.
3. Stir in the ground almonds, plain flour, almond extract, vanilla extract, and pinch of salt until just combined. Donâ€™t overmix.
