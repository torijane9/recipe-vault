## Garlic Sage Meatballs

### Ingredients
- [ ] 2 cloves garlic, minced
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
- [ ] 1 Tbsp sage, chopped
- [ ] 3/4 tsp kosher salt
- [ ] Black pepper
- [ ] 500g ground chicken mince
- [ ] Neutral oil
---
### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C.
2. In a mixing bowl, combine all of the ingredients except the oil. Stir until well combined.
3. Using a large spoon or cookie scooper, scoop out rounds of the meat mixture and roll into balls. Place on a lightly greased baking sheet.
4. Add neutral oil to a large skillet on high heat. Once the oil is shimmering and hot, add the meatballs. Cook each side until golden brown (about 2-3 minutes per side).
5. Place back on the greased baking sheet and finish cooking in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the meatballs are cooked through.
