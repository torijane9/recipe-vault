## Chicken Tinga

### Ingredients
- [ ] Neutral oil
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
- [ ] [[Tinga Braising Sauce]]
---
### Instructions
1. Lightly season the chicken breasts.
2. Preheat a shallow frying pan on high heat. Lightly coat the bottom of the pan with neutral oil
3. Add the chicken breasts and cook for 4-5 minutes, or until lightly seared. Flip the chicken, then immediately add in the tinga braising liquid. 
4. Cover with a lid, reduce to a simmer, and cook for 23-25 minutes.
5. Turn off the heat and rest for 10 minutes before shredding the chicken directly in the sauce.
