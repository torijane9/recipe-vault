### Creamed Paua

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] oil
- [ ] butter
- [ ] onions, chopped
- [ ] minced paua
- [ ] cream
- [ ] chicken soup packet (or onion soup packet if pescatarian)
- [ ] salt and pepper to taste
---
#### Instructions
1. Heat oil and butter in a frying pan on medium heat. 
2. Add onions and cook until fragrant and nearly cooked (until the onions are starting to get clear)
3. Add paua and fry together. 
4. Pour in cream, once heated through let it simmer for 5 minutes then throw in the soup mix packet and mix together.
5. Season to taste with salt and pepper
