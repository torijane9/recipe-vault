### Five-spice Roast Pork Belly 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] Approx 750g skin-on pork belly
- [ ] 2 tsp salt
- [ ] 1 tsp Chinese five-spice powder
---
#### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Place the pork belly on an oven rack in a roasting pan, skin side up. 
2. Combine the salt and five-spice powder and rub them into the skin of the pork.
3. Pour enough water into the pan to fill it to just under the rack. 
4. Roast the pork belly for 3 hours until the meat is very tender and the crackling is crispy. Top up the water as it is cooking if necessary.
