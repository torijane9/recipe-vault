### Sticky Pork 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 300g piece pork fillet
- [ ] 2 garlic cloves, crushed
- [ ] 1 small knob fresh ginger, grated
- [ ] 1/4 cup kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
- [ ] 2 tsp sesame oil
- [ ] 1 Tbsp neutral oil
---
#### Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl, ensuring the pork is well covered. Glad wrap and chill.
2. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Grease and line a baking tray with baking paper. 
3. Heat a pan over a medium heat, and add the neutral oil. Drain the pork and discard marinate. Fry on all sides for 5-6 minutes or until seared and golden.
4. Transfer pork to the prepared baking tray and roast for 10-12 minutes or until just cooked through. Cover lightly with foil and set aside for 10 minutes before slicing into preferred serving sizes.
