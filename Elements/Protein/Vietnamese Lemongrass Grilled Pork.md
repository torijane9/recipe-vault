### Vietnamese Grilled Lemongrass Pork

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 900g pork shoulder, sliced to about 6mm thick slices
Marinade
- [ ] 6 garlic cloves
- [ ] 1 large shallot
- [ ] 2 stalks lemongrass (cut off top and bottom and use the inner part only)
- [ ] 1/4 cup fish sauce
- [ ] 3 Tbsp neutral oil
- [ ] Black pepper to taste
Sauce
- [ ] 3 tsp honey
- [ ] 1 tsp water
To Serve
- [ ] Fresh lime juice (as much as you like)
---
#### Instructions
1. Place the marinade ingredients into a food processor and process to a fine mixture.
2. Pour the marinade sauce over the sliced pork and marinate overnight (or up to 24 hours) in the fridge.
3. Before you are going to grill the pork, remove from the fridge and leave at room temperature for 30 minutes.
4. Preheat grill over medium-high to high heat and grill each piece of pork for about 3-4 minutes per side.
5. Mix the sauce ingredients together and brush on each pork slice, then grill for 2 more minutes per side until the meat is cooked through and the surface is a light golden brown.
6. Serve with fresh lime juice and eat while sizzling hot
