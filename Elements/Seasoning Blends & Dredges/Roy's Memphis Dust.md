### Roy’s Memphis Dust 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 3/4 cup brown sugar
- [ ] 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- [ ] 1/2 cup smoked paprika
- [ ] 1/4 cup kosher salt
- [ ] 1/4 cup garlic powder
- [ ] 2 Tbsp ground black pepper
- [ ] 2 Tbsp ginger powder
- [ ] 2 Tbsp onion powder
- [ ] 2 Tbsp rosemary powder
---
#### Instructions
1. Thoroughly combine all ingredients together.

#### Notes:
- Can be stored in an airtight jar for ages, but doesn’t last that long anyway!
- Delicious on any meats, especially pork ribs/pork belly
