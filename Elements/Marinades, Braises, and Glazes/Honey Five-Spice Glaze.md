## Honey Five-Spice Meat Glaze

### Ingredients
- [ ] 3 Tbsp honey
- [ ] 2 Tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
- [ ] 1 tsp brown sugar
- [ ] 3/4 tsp Chinese five-spice powder
---
### Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan. Stir over low heat until sugar dissolves, about 3 minutes.

#### Notes
- This is especially delicious on duck or chicken, but can also be used on pork belly/ribs. It would also probably be delicious on grilled tofu!
