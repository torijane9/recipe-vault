## Yellow Coconut Curry Base

### Ingredients
- [ ] Neutral oil
- [ ] 1/3 yellow onion, or 1 shallot, finely diced
- [ ] 1 tsp kosher salt, plus a pinch
- [ ] 2 tsp curry powder
- [ ] 1 tsp garam masala
- [ ] 1 thumb-sized knob of ginger, grated
- [ ] 1 serrano chilli, thinly sliced
- [ ] 1 clove of garlic, minced
- [ ] 1 can coconut milk
- [ ] 1/2 cup chicken stock or water
- [ ] 1/4 tsp turmeric
- [ ] 1 tsp brown sugar
---
### Instructions
1. Heat a 1-2 tbsp neutral oil in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and a pinch of salt and saut√© until slightly caramelised (around 7-8 minutes).
2. Add curry powder, garam masala, ginger, serrano chilli, and garlic, and cook an additional 1-2 minutes, just until slightly toasted and fragrant.
3. Slowly stir in coconut milk, stock, turmeric, brown sugar, and 1 tsp of salt. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes. 
4. Turn off the heat and allow the curry to cool down slightly before blending until smooth.
5. Transfer to an air-tight container and store in the refrigerator for later use.
