## 30-Minute Cheat Rotisserie Chicken Stock

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1 rotisserie chicken carcass, bones, and chicken skin
- [ ] 1 onion, diced
- [ ] 1 large carrot, diced
- [ ] 8 cups water
- [ ] 1/4 cup parsley (stems included)
---
### Instructions
1. Place all ingredients in a large pot over medium-low heat. Cook until simmering (small, infrequent bubbles will appear on the sides of the pot). Reduce the heat to low and cook for at least 30 minutes and up to 2 hours.
2. Strain the stock into a large container and set aside until using.
