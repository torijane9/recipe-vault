### Tomato Braised Chickpeas

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 2 Tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 1 red onion, chopped
- [ ] 3 cups chicken or vegetable stock
- [ ] 2x 400g cans chopped tomatoes
- [ ] 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- [ ] 400g can chickpeas, drained
- [ ] salt and pepper, to taste
---
#### Instructions
1. Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan over low heat, and gently fry the onion until soft.
2. Add the stock, tomatoes, and garlic. Turn the heat to high, and cook uncovered until the stock is reduced by about half, and the tomatoes are pulpy.
3. Add the chickpeas, and simmer for another 10 minutes. 
4. Season to taste.
