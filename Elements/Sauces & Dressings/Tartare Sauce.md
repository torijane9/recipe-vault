## Tartare Sauce

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1 lot of [[Mayonnaise From Scratch]]
- [ ] 2 Tbsp finely chopped gherkins
- [ ] 1 Tbsp capers, rinsed and finely chopped
- [ ] 2 Tbsp finely chopped fresh parsley
- [ ] 1 Tbsp finely chopped fresh dill (optional)
- [ ] 1 tsp finely chopped shallot or spring onion (optional)
- [ ] A squeeze of fresh lemon juice, to taste
- [ ] Pinch of black pepper
---
### Instructions
1. Mix all the ingredients into the base mayonnaise. 
2. For the best flavour, let it sit in the fridge for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavours to meld together.
