### Tangy Fish Sauce Dressing 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1/2 red onion cut into thin slices
- [ ] 1/2 cup fresh squeezed lime juice (from 4-6 limes)
- [ ] 1/4 cup fish sauce plus more to taste
- [ ] 2 Tbsp granulated sugar
- [ ] 1/4 cup boiling water
- [ ] 1-2 red chillies cut into thin slices
---
#### Instructions
1. Add sugar and boiling water to a medium sized heat-proof measuring cup or bowl. 
2. Carefully mix to dissolve the sugar completely.
3. Add the lime juice and fish sauce and mix to combine, then add the sliced chillies and onions. Set aside for 15-30 minutes.
4. Taste the sauce and add any extra fish sauce or lime juice to taste.
