### Chilli Butter

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 50g butter
- [ ] 1 Tbsp chilli flakes (Aleppo/Kashmiri/your favourite)
- [ ] 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] 1/4 tsp ground cumin
- [ ] 1 clove garlic, minced
---
#### Instructions
1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat; heat until bubbles begin to burst. 
2. Add chilli flakes, paprika, and cumin. Stir until colour is uniform. Turn off heat and let spices infuse.
