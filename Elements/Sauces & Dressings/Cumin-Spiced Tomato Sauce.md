### Cumin-Spiced Tomato Sauce 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] Olive oil
- [ ] 1/3 yellow onion or 1 large shallot, finely diced
- [ ] 1 tsp kosher salt + a small pinch
- [ ] 1 bell pepper, diced
- [ ] 2 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- [ ] 1/2 tsp ground paprika
- [ ] 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
- [ ] 1 can crushed tomatoes
- [ ] 1/4 cup water
- [ ] 1/8 tsp or a small pinch white sugar
---
#### Instructions
1. Heat 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large saucepan on medium-high heat.
2. Add diced onion along with a small pinch of salt and cook while periodically stirring for 3-4 minutes, or until mostly translucent.
3. Add diced bell pepper and cook for an additional 2 minutes or until mostly soft.
4. Add cumin , paprika , and crushed red pepper fl akes , and cook for around 30 seconds.
5. Add crushed tomatoes , water , sugar , and 1 tsp of kosher salt and stir until well combined.
6. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook for 25-30 minutes.
7. Transfer to an air-tight container and store in the refrigerator for later use.
