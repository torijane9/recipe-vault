## Tahini Date Dressing

### Ingredients
- [ ] 3 medjool dates (approx 70g), pitted
- [ ] 1/3 cup hot water
- [ ] 1/2 cup olive oil
- [ ] 2 Tbsp + 1 tsp (36g) red wine vinegar
- [ ] 1 Tbsp tahini
- [ ] 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- [ ] 1 clove garlic
---
### Instructions
1. In a bowl, place the dates in the hot water and soak for 15-20 minutes.
2. Transfer the soaked dates and water into a blender. Add the rest of the ingredients, and blend until smooth.
