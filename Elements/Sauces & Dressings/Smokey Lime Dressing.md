## Smokey Lime Dressing

### Ingredients
- [ ] Juice of 2 fresh limes (about 60ml/1/4 cup)
- [ ] Zest of 1 fresh lime
- [ ] Juice of 1/2 an orange (about 30ml/2 Tbsp)
- [ ] 2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- [ ] 1 clove garlic, minced
- [ ] 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] 1/4 tsp ground cumin
- [ ] 1 tsp maple syrup or agave nectar
- [ ] 1/2 tsp sea salt, or to taste
- [ ] 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper, or to taste
---
### Instructions
1. In a small bowl or jar, whisk together all of the ingredients until well combined and emulsified. 
2. Taste and adjust seasonings if necessary - you might want a tiny bit more salt, sweetness, or lime.
