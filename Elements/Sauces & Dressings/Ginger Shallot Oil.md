### Ginger Shallot Oil

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 2 cloves garlic, finely minced
- [ ] 1 large shallot (or 1/2 a small onion) finely minced
- [ ] 1 inch fresh ginger peeled and finely minced
- [ ] 1/2 cup neutral oil 
---
#### Instructions
1. Add minced aromatics to a medium sized heat-proof bowl. 
2. Heat neutral oil in a pot until just barely starting to smoke, then carefully pour it over the aromatics - be careful! It will sputter and splatter.
3. Mix gently and set aside to cool a bit.
