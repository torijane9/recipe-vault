### Herb Yoghurt

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1 cup plain unsweetened yoghurt
- [ ] 1 clove garlic, crushed
- [ ] 2 Tbsp lemon juice
- [ ] 1/3 cup mixed chopped fresh herbs (mint, basil, parsley, coriander etc)
---
#### Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients together in a bowl, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
