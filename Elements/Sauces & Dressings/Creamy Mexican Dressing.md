## Creamy Mexican Dressing

### Ingredients
- [ ] 120g (1/2 cup) good quality mayonnaise
- [ ] 120g (1/2 cup) sour cream (or full-fat Greek yoghurt for a lighter option)
- [ ] 2 Tbsp (30ml) fresh lime juice
- [ ] 1 clove garlic, minced
- [ ] 1 tsp (5g) ancho chilli powder (or a mix of 1/2 tsp smoked paprika and 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper)
- [ ] 1/2 tsp (2.5g) ground cumin
- [ ] 1/4 tsp (1.25g) fine sea salt, or to taste
---
### Instructions
1. In a bowl, whisk together all the ingredients until smooth and well combined. Give it a taste and adjust the seasoning if needed - you might want a little more lime for acidity or a little more salt.
