### Garlic Yoghurt Dip

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1 cup unsweetened Greek yoghurt
- [ ] 1 clove garlic, minced
- [ ] 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
- [ ] 1/4 tsp salt, or to taste
- [ ] chopped parsley
- [ ] chopped dill
---
#### Instructions
1. place yoghurt in a medium bowl and add in garlic; mix to combine. Season with pepper and salt. Add dill and parsley and mix thoroughly, and set aside at room temperature.
