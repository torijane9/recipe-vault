### Garlicky Yoghurt Sauce 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1 cup Greek yoghurt
- [ ] Juice of 1/2 lemon
- [ ] 2 cloves garlic, grated
- [ ] 1 Tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 3/4 tsp kosher salt
- [ ] 2 Tbsp water
---
#### Instructions
1. In a small mixing bowl, stir all ingredients together until fully combined.
