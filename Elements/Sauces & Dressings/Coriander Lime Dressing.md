## Coriander Lime Dressing

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1/2 cup coriander
- [ ] 1/4 garlic powder
- [ ] 1/2 cup greek yoghurt
- [ ] Juice of 1 lime
- [ ] 1 tsp distilled white vinegar
- [ ] 1 tsp honey
- [ ] 1/2 tsp salt
- [ ] 1/2 of a jalapeno
- [ ] 1 Tbsp neutral oil
---
### Instructions
1. In a blender or food processor, combine all ingredients and blend until fully emulsified
