### Element Name 

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] 1/4 cup (4 Tbsp) hoisin sauce
- [ ] 1 Tbsp water (or a little more to reach desired consistency)
- [ ] 1 tsp sesame oil
- [ ] 1/2 tsp rice vinegar
- [ ] 1/4 tsp chilli garlic paste/sauce (optional for some kick)
- [ ] Sprinkle of toasted sesame seeds for garnish
---
#### Instructions
1. Combine ingredients (except sesame seeds) in a small bowl and whisk together.
2. If the sauce is too thick, add a little more water, a tsp at a time, until it reaches your preferred dipping consistency.
3. Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds before serving
