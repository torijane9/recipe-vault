## Sumac Dressing

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- [ ] 1 medium lemon, juiced (about 3 Tbsp)
- [ ] 2 tsp sumac
- [ ] 1 tsp honey 
- [ ] Salt and pepper, to taste
---
### Instructions
1. In a bowl, whisk all of the ingredients together until combined and emulsified.
