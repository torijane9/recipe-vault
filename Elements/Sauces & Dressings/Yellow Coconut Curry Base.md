### Yellow Coconut Curry Base

### Ingredients
#ingredients 
- [ ] Neutral oil
- [ ] 1/3 yellow onion or one shallow, finely diced
- [ ] 1 tsp kosher salt, plus a pinch
- [ ] 2 tsp curry powder
- [ ] 1 tsp garam masala
- [ ] 1 inch knob of ginger, grated
- [ ] 1 serrano chilli, thinly sliced
- [ ] 1 clove of garlic, minced
- [ ] 1 (400ml) can of coconut milk
- [ ] 1/2 cup chicken stock or water
- [ ] 1/4 tsp turmeric
- [ ] 1 tsp brown sugar
---
#### Instructions
1. Heat a 1-2 tablespoons of neutral oil in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and a pinch of salt and sauté until slightly caramelized (around 7-8 minutes).
2. Add curry powder , garam masala , ginger , serrano , and garlic and sauté for an additional 1-2 minutes, just until slightly toasted and fragrant.
3. Slowly stir in coconut milk , stock , turmeric , brown sugar , and 1 tsp of kosher salt . Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
4. Turn off the heat and allow the curry to cool down slightly before blending until smooth.
5. Transfer to an air-tight container and store in the refrigerator for later use.
