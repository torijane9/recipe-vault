## Feta & Lemon Dressing

### Ingredients
- [ ] 3 Tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 2 Tbsp lemon juice
- [ ] 100g feta, crumbled
- [ ] 1/2 - 1 tsp honey
- [ ] salt and freshly ground black pepper
---
### Instructions
1. Combine the olive oil, lemon juice, feta, and honey in a bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste.
