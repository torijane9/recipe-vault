## Chipotle Sauce

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1-2 chipotle peppers from a can of chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
- [ ] 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- [ ] 1 Tbsp lime juice
- [ ] 1 tsp sugar
- [ ] 1/2 tsp salt
- [ ] garlic powder, to taste
- [ ] onion powder, to taste
- [ ] paprika, to taste
---
### Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients together in a blender, and blend until smooth.
