## Lemonade Syrup

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- [ ] 1 1/2 cups water
- [ ] Zest of 1 lemon
- [ ] 1 1/2 cups fresh lemon juice
---
### Instructions
1. Measure the sugar and water into a saucepan. Stir with a wooden spoon and bring to a boil for 5 minutes.
2. Remove from the heat, let cool slightly, then add the lemon juice and lemon zest.
3. Cool completely, and pour into a jar or bottle. 

#### Notes
- Can be kept in the fridge for 2-3 weeks.
