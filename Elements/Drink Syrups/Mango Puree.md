## Mango Puree

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1 mango, pitted and chopped
- [ ] 1 1/2 cups water
- [ ] 1/2 cup sugar
- [ ] pinch of salt
---
### Instructions
1. In a small pan, combine ingredients together. Cook for about 3 minutes.
2. Blend together until smooth.
