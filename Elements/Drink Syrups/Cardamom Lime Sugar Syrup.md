## Cardamom Sugar Syrup

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1 cup cane sugar or grated palm sugar
- [ ] 1 kaffir lime leaf, crushed
- [ ] 1 Tbsp lime zest
- [ ] 1 green cardamom pod, lightly crushed
- [ ] 1 cup water
---
### Instructions
1. In a small saucepan, combine the ingredients together and bring to a boil.
2. Simmer over low heat for 10 minutes, or until the mixture is reduced to a thin syrup.
3. Remove the lime leaf and cardamom, allow the sugar syrup to cool, and then chill it.
