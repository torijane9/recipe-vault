## Hibiscus Syrup

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1 cup granulated sugar
- [ ] 1 cup water
- [ ] 1/2 cup dried hibiscus flowers
---
### Instructions
1. In a small pot, combine the ingredients. Stir together and bring to a low boil.
2. Boil for just a few minutes until all the sugar is dissolved.
3. Take off the heat and allow to cool. Strain hibiscus flowers from the syrup.
4. You can keep the steeped flowers as a garnish for drinks - store these in an airtight container or ziplock bag in the fridge until using.

#### Notes
- Refrigerate for up to a week in a sealed jar.
