## Peach Wine Slushy

### Ingredients
- [ ] 2 cups cold white wine
- [ ] Half a bag of frozen peaches (or about 2-3 medium peeled and sliced peaches, frozen)
- [ ] Sliced peaches, for garnish
---
### Instructions
1. In a blender, combine the wine and frozen peaches. Puree until smooth. 
2. If the mixture is too warm and liquefied, place it in the freezer and stir every 30 minutes until the perfect slushy texture forms.

#### Notes
- You can double this recipe and use the entire bottle of wine to serve 4.
