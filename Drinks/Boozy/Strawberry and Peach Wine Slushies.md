## Strawberry and Peach Wine Slushies

### Ingredients
- [ ] 2 1/2 cups frozen strawberries
- [ ] 2 1/2 cups frozen peaches
- [ ] 1 1/2 cups white wine of choice
- [ ] Slices of strawberry and peach, to garnish
- [ ] Mint leaves, to garnish
---
### Instructions
1. In a blender, combine frozen strawberries with 3/4 cup white wine and blend until smooth. You can add more wine if you want a thinner slushy.
2. Pour the strawberry wine slush evenly between 4 glasses.
3. Rince the blender and add frozen peaches and remaining white wine. Blend until smooth.
4. Spoon the peach wine slush over the strawberry slush. 
5. Garnish with strawberry slices, peach slices, and mint leaves, and serve!
