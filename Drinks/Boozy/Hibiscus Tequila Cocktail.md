## Hibiscus Tequila Cocktail

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1/2 cup Tequila
- [ ] 6 Tbsp [[Hibiscus Syrup]]
- [ ] 6 Tbsp lime juice, fresh squeezed
- [ ] Ice
- [ ] Lime slices, to garnish
- [ ] Hibiscus flowers, to garnish (optional)
---
### Instructions
1. Add tequila, hibiscus syrup, and lime juice to a cocktail shaker. You can add more syrup if youâ€™d like it sweeter.
2. Add ice to shaker. Close lid and shake thoroughly. Pour into glasses with or without ice.
3. Garnish with hibiscus flowers and wedge of lime.
