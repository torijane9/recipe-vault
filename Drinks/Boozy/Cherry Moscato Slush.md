## Cherry Moscato Slush

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1 bottle Moscato wine, chilled
- [ ] 600g frozen pitted cherries
- [ ] Lime wedges, to garnish (optional)
---
### Instructions
1. Add the wine and frozen cherries to a blender, and blend until smooth.
2. Pour into four glasses. If you donâ€™t want the cherry skins, you can strain while pouring into the glasses.
3. Garnish each with a slice of lime, and enjoy.
