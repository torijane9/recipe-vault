## Frozen Lemon Cocktail

### Ingredients
- [ ] 30ml vodka
- [ ] 30ml limoncello
- [ ] 1 scoop lemon sorbet
- [ ] 1 splash prosecco
- [ ] lemon slice, to garnish
---
### Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix with a whisk
2. Pour the mixture into a glass and garnish with lemon slice
