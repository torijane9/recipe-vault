## Mango Coconut Crush

### Ingredients
- [ ] 120ml vodka
- [ ] 1/2 cup [[Mango Puree]]
- [ ] 1/2 cup coconut milk
- [ ] 4 Tbsp orange juice
- [ ] ice
- [ ] lime wedges
---
### Instructions
1. Fill two glasses with ice cubes and add the vodka.
2. In a separate pitcher, mix together the mango puree, coconut milk, and then pour it over the ice and vodka. 
3. Top with orange juice and squeeze of lime.
