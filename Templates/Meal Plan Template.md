### Weekly Meal Plan: Asian-Inspired Freshness with Flexible Components

**Theme:** This week focuses on vibrant Southeast Asian and Japanese flavours, utilising versatile prepped components to create diverse meals with minimal daily effort. We're leaning into fresh produce and adaptable proteins.

**Key Prepped Components:** Toasted Coconut Rice, Honey Lime Grilled Salmon (for Lia), Five-Spice Roast Pork Belly (for Tori), Vietnamese-style Pickled Vegetables, Peanut Satay Sauce, Fresh Coriander & Mint Mix.

---

**Monday**
*   **Breakfast (Lia):** Sweet Coconut Breakfast Rice Porridge with fresh mango.
*   **Breakfast (Tori):** Nutty Muesli Clusters with Greek yoghurt.
*   **Lunch (Both - at home):** Leftover Vietnamese Salad Bowls (pork for Tori, salmon/tofu for Lia) with fresh herbs.
*   **Dinner (Shared):** Honey Lime Grilled Salmon (Lia) / Five-Spice Roast Pork Belly (Tori) with Toasted Coconut Rice and a quick stir-fried greens side.

**Tuesday**
*   **Breakfast (Lia):** Lia's Anti-Inflammatory Muesli with berries.
*   **Breakfast (Tori):** Nutty Muesli Clusters with Greek yoghurt.
*   **Lunch (Both - work):** Vietnamese Salad Bowls with prepped components (pork for Tori, salmon/tofu for Lia), fresh herbs, and rice vermicelli.
*   **Dinner (Shared):** Teriyaki Salmon (Lia) / Teriyaki Chicken (Tori) with Coconut Rice and steamed pak choy.

**Wednesday**
*   **Breakfast (Lia):** Sweet Coconut Breakfast Rice Porridge with toasted nuts.
*   **Breakfast (Tori):** Nutty Muesli Clusters with Greek yoghurt.
*   **Lunch (Lia - campus):** Vietnamese Salad Bowl (Tofu/Salmon) with Peanut Satay Dressing.
*   **Lunch (Tori - office):** Leftover Teriyaki Salmon/Chicken with Coconut Rice.
*   **Dinner (Shared):** Deconstructed Sushi Bowls: Toasted Coconut Rice, Shredded Honey Lime Salmon (Lia) / Shredded Teriyaki Chicken (Tori), avocado, cucumber, nori strips, and a drizzle of sriracha mayo.

**Thursday**

*   **Breakfast (Tori):** Nutty Muesli Clusters with Greek yoghurt.
*   **Lunch (Both - at home):** Quick Tofu Stir-fry (Lia) / Pork Belly Stir-fry (Tori) with leftover rice and some prepped veggies.
*   **Dinner (Shared):** Mexican-inspired Fish Parcels (Lia) / Chicken & Black Bean Tacos (Tori) with Avocado Cucumber Salsa and fresh coriander.

**Friday**
*   **Breakfast (Lia):** Sweet Coconut Breakfast Rice Porridge with fresh fruit.
*   **Breakfast (Tori):** Nutty Muesli Clusters with Greek yoghurt.
*   **Lunch (Both - work):** Leftover Mexican Fish Parcels / Tacos (deconstructed in containers).
*   **Dinner:** Takeaway!

**Saturday**
*   **Breakfast (Relaxed):** Herbed Mini Pancakes with a side of smoked salmon (Lia) / crispy bacon (Tori) and a dollop of crème fraîche.
*   **Lunch (Casual):** Leftover components or quick wraps with whatever is on hand.
*   **Dinner (Shared):** Chermoula Roast Chicken (Tori) / Baked Snapper with Chermoula (Lia) with a vibrant Kale and Apple Bulgar Wheat Salad.

**Sunday**
*   **Breakfast (Relaxed):** Lia's Anti-Inflammatory Muesli with extra nuts and seeds.

*   **Dinner (Shared):** Hearty Cannellini Bean Soup with crusty bread (vegetarian for both, but Tori could add some leftover pork if desired).
